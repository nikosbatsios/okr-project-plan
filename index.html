Project Timeline

The project will be delivered in two phases to ensure rapid delivery of core functionality and value.

Phase 1: Foundation & Automation

Target: End of July 2025

Deliverables:

  • Deployment of the 3 Microsoft Lists.
  • Core Power Automate workflow for progress roll-up.

Phase 2: Analytics & Visualization

Target: August 2025

Deliverables:

  • Development of Power BI dashboards.
  • User training and documentation.
